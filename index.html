Australian Exports per Country

This dashboard, created by Nathan Marlow, a student at Monash University Australia, displays Australia's exports to various countries and from various states from 1988 till 2023.

This map demonstrates Australia's exports to different countries over the years from 1988 till 2023. The sliders can be used to change between various years 1988 to 2023 and the month slider between each month of the year

*Note Belgium and Lexumbourg share the same values until 1/07/2003. Sudan uses the values of "Former Sudan" until 1/10/2012. Russia uses the values for the USSR until 1/09/1992. France includes all territories and 'Wallis and Futuna' Hong Kong and Macau is included in China's total. The Federated states of Micronesia, Samoa and the United States Miscellaneous Islands, Virgin Islands, United States are included in the USA's total. The Netherlands Antilles are included in the Netherlands' total. Polynesia is included in New Zealand's total. Serbia and Montenegro share the same data until 01/05/2008. Southern and East Africa is included in South Africa's total. Virgin Islands, British, Turks and Caicos Islands are included in the United Kingdom's total. Australian Antarctic Territory is included in Australia's total.
